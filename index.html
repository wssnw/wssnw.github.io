THE LEGACY

This vision was born from the streets of Da Hood.

I started crafting this world alone at just 13 years old. What began as a dream in 2024 was supposed to meet the world in 2025. But time is a heavy thing.

The release shifted to 2026, and now, as life pulls me in different directions, 2028 has become the final destination.

Quality takes time. Life takes priority. The fog remains.
