Foggy Fights

How it started

I grew up playing Da Hood and always wanted to make something similar but my own way. I started Foggy Fights back in 2025 when I was still really young.

Everything you see in the game was made by me—I didn't use free models or a big dev team. It’s just been a solo project from day one.

Release is set for 2028.

The Gameplay

The Fight

It's straightforward. You see someone, you fight them. No complicated rules, just making a name for yourself.

The Vibe

Everything is based on that 2025 street-edit style. It's meant to look and feel like those old clips.
