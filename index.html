Foggy Fights

// My Story

I grew up playing Da Hood and always wanted to make something similar but in my own style. I started Foggy Fights back in 2025 when I was still pretty young.

I made everything myself—no free models and no team helping me out. It's just been me working on this since day one.

Release date is 2028.

// The Game

The game is simple: kill who you see and make a name for yourself. Everything is based on that 2025 street-edit culture you used to see.
